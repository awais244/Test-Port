About Me
Hi, I’m Awais—a UX Design student with a passion for creating intuitive, meaningful, and engaging experiences that connect people with technology. With a strong academic foundation in UX and game design, I specialize in blending creativity and problem-solving to design solutions that are both user-centered and innovative. My interests extend into information architecture and research, where I explore concepts like LATCH and DIKW to build organized, functional designs that resonate with users.
In addition to my academic pursuits, I enjoy working on creative projects, such as book cover designs and interactive portfolio pieces, that showcase my attention to detail and commitment to excellence. I thrive in collaborative environments, leveraging diverse perspectives and research-driven strategies to deliver impactful results.
When I’m not designing, you’ll often find me at the beach with my dog, reading, or exploring new ways to bring creativity into my everyday life. Let’s connect and work together to turn ideas into experiences that make a difference!
